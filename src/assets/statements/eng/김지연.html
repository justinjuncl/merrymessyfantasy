1.

A small hut made of pillows by a little child,

The body of a tortoise with its head and four legs hidden,

A blanket covering head to toe to avoid the winter cold,

When something painful happens,

I crouch my body.

When I want to cry,

I lock the door to my room and hold my breath in a corner.

Isolating myself from the outside, separating the space where I am.

Like a tortoise pulling its head into its shell,

I hide just like that.

2.

The act of confining myself yields security.

The isolated space separates me from the painful reality.

It will lead me to the paradise I have found from where I have fled.

Life is not kind, and I am ready to run away.

Disconnected from the reality,

let me find comfort in a space of my own without any suffering.

Let me run farther away.

To a place where the reality cannot find me.

To Seocheon.
