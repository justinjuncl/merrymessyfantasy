“Commodities are no longer defined by their use, but rather by what they signify.”

- French philosopher, Jean Baudrillard <La Société de Consommation(1970)>

The meaning of economic exchange, purchasing and selling commodities, has expanded in modern times. Today, it includes "the behavior of producing and consuming symbols." Beyond the optimistic or pessimistic perspective, whether we agree with it or criticize it, now is the era when the terms of production and consumption overlaps the concept of communication.

Here we face two unfamiliar facts.

1. The concepts of production and consumption signify communication.

2. Although people produce and consume materials, what they are actually producing and consuming are symbols.

This artwork, the project ‘SELL YOUR MIND’, visualizes various original ideas (thoughts, opinions, and emotions) written in short writings. The next version of this project would be in the form of a shopping mall that enables producing and purchasing the ideas that are visualized just like this first version of the project.

Wouldn’t you join in the buying and selling of the idea-symbols?
